Homemade Wheat Bread

Freshly baked loaf of homemade wheat bread

This homemade bread recipe is made with honey as a source of sugar and organic white flour. Makes two loaves.

Ingredients

6 cups whole wheat flour

2 cups warm water

1/2 cup warm milk

1/4 cup honey

1 1/2 tablespoons instant yeast

2 tablespoons cooking oil

1 tablespoon salt

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, add warm water and milk with honey.

2. Whisk until combined

3. Sprinkle yeast on top

4. Let sit for 5+ minutes

5. Add oil, salt and 4 cups of flour

6. Stir until incorporated

7. Incorporate rest of flour 1/2 cup at a time until dough begins to pull away from bowl

8. Knead dough for 7 minutes

9. Transfer dough to an oil coated bowl, cover, and let rise for 60 minutes

10. Punch down dough, cover, and let rise for 30 minutes

11. Divide dough in two loaves and transfer to two well oiled loaf pans

12. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise for 30 minutes

13. Preheat oven to 350 degrees

14. Bake for 30 minutes

15. Take bread out of the oven and coat with butter while cooling
