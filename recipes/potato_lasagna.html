Potato and Ham Lasagna

This potato and ham lasagna recipe is made from layers of sliced potato, ham, and shredded cheese. Heavy whipping cream and egg are used as a binding agent.

Ingredients

5 potatos

8 slices ham

Shredded mozzerella

Shredded cheddar

1 cup heavy cream

2 eggs

2 teaspoons salt

1 teaspoon pepper

Frank's Red Hot sauce

Asiago cheese

Instructions

1. Cut potato into 1/4 inch slices.

2. In a bowl, season potatos with salt and pepper

3. Add a layer of potatos to the bottom of an oiled square baking pan

4. Place 4 ham slices on top of the first layer of potatos

5. Add a layer of shredded mozzerella and cheddar cheese

6. Add another layer of potato

7. Add another layer of ham

8. Add another layer of shredded cheese

9. Preheat oven to 350 degress

10. Add Frank's Red Hot sauce

11. Add sprinkling of asiago cheese to the top

12. In a small bowl, mix the heavy cream and eggs

13. Poor the mixed heavy cream and eggs on top

14. Bake for 40 minutes
