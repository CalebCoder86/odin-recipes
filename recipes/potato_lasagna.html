Potato and Ham Lasagna

This potato and ham lasagna recipe is made from layers of sliced potato, ham, and shredded cheese. Heavy whipping cream and egg are used as a binding agent.

Ingredients

5 potatos

8 slices ham

Shredded mozzerella

Shredded cheddar

1 cup heavy cream

2 eggs

1 onion

2 teaspoons salt

1 teaspoon pepper

Frank's Red Hot sauce

Asiago cheese

Green onion

Instructions

1. Cut potato into 1/4 inch slices.

2. Dice onion.

3. Boil potatos until soft

4. Add a layer of potatos to the bottom of an oiled square baking pan

5. Place 4 ham slices on top of the first layer of potatos

6. Add a layer of shredded mozzerella, shredded cheddar cheese and diced onion

7. Add Frank's Red Hot sauce.

8. Add another layer of potato

9. Add another layer of ham

10. Add a layer of shredded cheese, diced onion, and green onion

11. Preheat oven to 350 degress

12. Add Frank's Red Hot sauce

13. Add sprinkling of asiago cheese to the top

14. In a small bowl, mix the heavy cream and eggs

15. Poor the mixed heavy cream and eggs on top

16. Bake for 40 minutes
