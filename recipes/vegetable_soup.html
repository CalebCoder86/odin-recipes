Vegetable Soup

This vegetable soup is made with vegetable broth, onions, potato's, carrots, canned corn and spices. Sour cream and wheat flour are used as a thickening agent.

Ingredients

1 large onion

3 large potatos

4 large carrots

2 bunches of green onion

1 can of corn

Vegetable broth

Minced garlic

Salt

Cooking oil

Spice of choice

Sour cream

Wheat flour

Black pepper

Instructions

1. Dice onion, potatos, carrots and green onion

2. Saute onion in oil until browned

3. Add potato and cook for 5 minutes

4. Add carrot and cook for 5 minutes

5. Add can of corn including liquid and cook for 5 minutes

6. Add 4/5 of veggie broth and bring to a boil

7. Add diced green onion

8. Add two tablespoons of minced garlic

9. Add two teaspoons of salt

10. Add spice of choice

11. Mix 1 tablespoon of wheat flour with remainder of vegetable broth

12. Cover and cook for 15 minutes

13. Add two tablespoons of sour cream

14. Add vegetable broth and wheat flour combination

15. Add pepper to taste

16. Cook for 5 minutes
