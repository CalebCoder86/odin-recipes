Homemade Wheat Bread

Freshly baked loaf of banana bread

This healthy bread recipe is made with honey as a source of sugar, coconut oil, and organic whole grain flour. Makes two loaves.

Ingredients

2/3 cup coconut oil

1 cup honey

4 eggs

4 bananas

1/2 cup milk

2 teaspoons baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

3 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

1. In a large bowl, add coconut oil and honey, then whisk until combined

2. Add eggs and whisk until combined

3. Add mashed bananas and milk, then whisk until combined

4. Add baking soda, salt, ground cinnamon, and vanilla, then whisk until combined

5. Stir in flour with a big spoon until combined

6. Poor batter into two oiled loaf pans

7. Sprinkle top of loaves with cinnamon

8. Preheat oven to 325 degrees

9. Bake for 50 minutes

10. Take bread out of the oven and let cool
