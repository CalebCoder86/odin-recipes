Seven Herb Tea

Black or Green tea that encorporates seven herbs with milk and honey

Ingredients

1/2 cup black or green tea

1 tablespoon Ginger

1 tablespoon Ashwaganda

1 teaspoon Turmeric

1 teaspoon Carraway seed

1 teaspoon Eucalyptus

1 teaspoon Vana (holy) basil

1 teaspoon Allspice

Milk

Honey

Instructions

1. Add tea to a small bowl (do not grind)

2. Grind Ginger and add to bowl

3. Grind Ashwaganda and add to bowl

4. Grind Carraway seed and add to bowl

5. Grind Eucalyptus and add to bowl

6. Grind Turmeric and add to bowl

7. Grind Allspice and add to bowl

8. Grind Vana and add to bowl

9. Stir until tea and herbs are evenly distributed

10. Add tea to steeper and place in tea cup

11. Boil water

12. Pour boiled water over steeper

13. Steep for 7 minutes

14. Add milk and honey
